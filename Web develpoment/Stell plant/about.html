About Us

Sets Steel Plant is one of the fastest growing Business Conglomerates in the eastern region of India, pioneer in manufacturing of integrated Iron & Steel Products, Cement, Power and Ferro Alloys. Led by Mr. Sajjan Kumar Patwari and his three sons Mr. Sunil Kumar Patwari, Mr. Sanjib Kumar Patwari and Mr. Sanjay Kumar Patwari, the group has its corporate office at Kolkata, and factories located at Kharagpur and Jhargram respectively

Its products and services range from DI Pipes, TMT Bars, Pig Iron, Wire Rods, MS Billets, Sponge Iron, Sinter, Ferro Alloys, Pellet Plant and Cement to Laptops, Netbooks, All-In-One PCs and also Dredging. It exports 4 billion TPA Iron Ore Fines to China & South East Asian countries, and mineral based products to other parts of the world. It has been awarded “Ultra Mega Project” status by West Bengal government and is among the first few to receive the environmental and pollution clearance in the state.

Mission
